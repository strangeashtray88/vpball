VP-Ball

VP-Ball is highly inspired by classics such as Breakout, Arcanoid, or Dx-Ball. Your goal is to clear the blocks, while not dropping the ball. You can pick up various extras, that makes the process easier or harder. Make your way through 40 levels and set new records by earning points. With its simple, yet addictive gameplay, charming graphics, and retro sound effects, this game is perfect for quick sessions.
